# **App Name**: Nordiska Solutions Website

## Core Features:

- Home Page: Display key information about Nordiska Solutions, including their mission, vision, and values.
- About Us: Detailed information about the company's history, team, and expertise.
- Solutions Showcase: Highlight Nordiska Solutions' cleantech offerings with descriptions, images, and case studies.
- Contact Form: Enable visitors to reach out to Nordiska Solutions with inquiries and requests.
- News and Insights: A blog or news section with articles, reports, and insights related to cleantech and Nordiska Solutions' work.
- Static Site Generation: Configuration for exporting the Next.js app as a static site.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep sea blue (#2E4A62) to reflect trustworthiness and a connection to natural resources.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F4F8), offering a clean, uncluttered backdrop.
- Accent color: Sunset orange (#D97335) to provide pops of energy and highlight calls to action.
- Body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, for clear and modern readability of body text.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' a proportional sans-serif with a techy feel for short amounts of impactful text.
- Use clean, minimalist icons to represent different solutions and aspects of the company's work.
- Implement a grid-based layout to ensure content is well-organized and easy to navigate.
- Subtle, tasteful animations on page transitions and when loading content to improve user experience.
